Overview

Inose Group Corporation is a Japan-based company engaged in asset management, proprietary investment activities, real estate investment, and IT-related business operations.

The company manages its operations with an emphasis on compliance, transparency, prudent risk management, and long-term capital allocation.

Business Activities

  • Asset management and proprietary investment
  • Real estate investment, ownership, and management
  • IT-related planning, development, and operational support
